Chris Hemsworth says 'I became a parody of myself' in 'Thor: Love and Thunder'

Chris Hemsworth, on the cover of Vanity Fair, expresses regret over "Thor: Love and Thunder" and desires a better "Thor" movie. Despite feeling replaceable within the Marvel team, Hemsworth remains grateful for the role and plans to explore other genres, including collaborations with renowned filmmakers such as Christopher Nolan and Steven Spielberg.
