'Mufasa: The Lion King' trailer: Blue Ivy Carter joins voice cast

Disney's "Mufasa: The Lion King" prequel explores Mufasa's origin with Blue Ivy Carter as Kiara. Directed by Barry Jenkins, the film features returning voice cast and introduces new members like Mads Mikkelsen, Thandiwe Newton, and Keith David. Lin-Manuel Miranda penned the songs, while Jeff Nathanson returns as screenwriter.
