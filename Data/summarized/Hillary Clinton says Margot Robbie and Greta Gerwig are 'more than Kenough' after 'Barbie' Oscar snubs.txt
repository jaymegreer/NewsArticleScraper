Hillary Clinton says Margot Robbie and Greta Gerwig are 'more than Kenough' after 'Barbie' Oscar snubs

Hillary Clinton joins the criticism of the Oscar's omission of Margot Robbie and Greta Gerwig for their work in "Barbie." Despite the film's box office success and multiple nominations, Robbie and Gerwig were not recognized in their respective acting and directing categories.
