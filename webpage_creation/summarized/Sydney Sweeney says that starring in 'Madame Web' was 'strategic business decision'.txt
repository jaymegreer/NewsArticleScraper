Sydney Sweeney says that starring in 'Madame Web' was 'strategic business decision'

Sydney Sweeney's role in "Madame Web" was a strategic business move for her Sony connections. Despite its box office failure, it paved the way for her success in "Anyone But You" and the upcoming "Barbarella" remake. Sweeney emphasizes the importance of producing her own projects to gain greater creative control and avoid experiences like "Madame Web," which she describes as restrictive.
